Company Profile

Phoenix is India’s fastest growing, audio and accessories brand catering to the youth. Phoenix was
recognised as the most Prestigious Rising Brand Of Asia 2023-2024 by BARC Asia in the gaming lifestyle accessories
category

Phoenix is on a mission to create a pop culture led brand that the youth is proud to own and Nodwin, a subsidiary of
Nazara Technologies, has a strategic investment in alignment with the long term vision.

Phoenix is led by its co-founders Adarsh Yadav (formerly Operations Head) and Mayur Shukla (formerly
Category Head for Headphones and Earbuds), both with a wealth of experience in the audio category.
